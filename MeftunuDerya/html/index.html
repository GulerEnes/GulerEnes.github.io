Who We Are?

Unmanned underwater vehicles are produced to fulfill the tasks determined under the sea in areas where it is difficult for humans to reach. Its main areas of use are reconnaissance, search and rescue, various researches, military actions. Meftun-u Derya team is a team that produces unmanned underwater vehicles and solutions for the design of these vehicles. Our team, which consists of a group of 10 people from different universities and is not affiliated with any university, was awarded a third place, a second place, and the most original software award in the Teknofest competitions it entered twice.

Our Awards

  • Teknofest 2022 AUV Most Original Software Award
